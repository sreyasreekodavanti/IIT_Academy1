OUR MANAGEMENT

Good management is the art of making problems so interesting and their solutions so constructive that everyone wants to get to work and deal with them.

Learning is a continuous process which should be supported with best and foremost facilities, so that, not only better performance is achieved and best output is yielded but also eminent professionals are gifted to the Nation. It was our founder's strong belief that took its shape in 1979 as a small and single branched institution - NARAYANA. He dreamt of extending it throughout India as he felt it should be accessible to large number of pupils and it is the only way to produce largest number of Professionals.


We take privilege in partnering in the progress of the Nation by gifting it with quality Professionals for the past 43 years and gifting golden careers to millions of students since Inception.


Asia's largest educational conglomerate with 400,000 students, 40,000 employees capital over 608 center's spread across 13 states, narayana is the pride of India. Narayana group consists of 300 junior colleges 300 schools along with 8 Professional colleges. With Three and half decades of experience, meticulous study material prepared by experts and exemplary teaching methodology, the education system of Narayana is matchless in delivering amazing results. With a set of courses that cadre to the needs and aspirations of the students, Narayana has already become a benchmark of academic excellence.


Armed with Micro Schedule that defines every hour of preparation, Narayana endeavors relentlessly to bring out the best of every student. Perfect academic planning make it easy for the teachers to complete the syllabus in advance and sufficient time is available for the student to prepare for the exam patterns. The progress of every student is closely monitored throughout the year and necessary changes are made to teaching and preparatory methods to suit his or her ability. With this huge support from the institute, no wonder Narayana students excel in competitive examinations at national level and secure admissions in prestigious Institutions like IIT's, NIT's, BIT-SAT,AIIMS,AFMC,BHU,JIMPER and other deemed Universities etc.
